Refund Policy

In the event a request for refund is made by the User for duplicate, incomplete or rejected transactions, as the case may be, the User agrees that F2 Fintech refund such amounts as may be decided by F2 Fintech in its sole discretion from time to time. Also such refund shall be made in accordance with the terms and conditions of the relevant service provider providing the payment gateway services.

Valid Reasons

  • Technical issues preventing the use of software or applications.
  • Cancellation of a subscription within the stipulated period.
  • Compliance with Terms of Use

Return and Refund Procedure

  1. Contact Customer Support: Initiate the return/refund process by contacting our customer support team via email at wecare@f2fintech.com. In your email, please include the following information:
    • Your full name.
    • Details of the product or service purchased.
    • Reason for the return/refund request.
    • Transaction details (order number, date of purchase).
  2. Evaluation: Once your return/refund request is received, our customer support team will evaluate your request based on the eligibility criteria mentioned above.
  3. Notification: You will be notified of the status of your return/refund request within 7 business days of your request submission. We will communicate our decision and the next steps.
  4. Return and Refund Processing: If your return/refund request is approved, we will process the return and refund within 7 business days. Refunds will be issued via the same payment method used for the original purchase.
